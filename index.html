Solo

Debut solo single of South Korean singer, rapper, and Blackpink member Jennie. Released on November 12, 2018, through YG and Interscope, the song was written by Teddy Park and produced by him alongside 24. Musically, "Solo" is a dance, pop, and hip hop song with EDM elements. Its lyrical content revolves around the theme of independence after a break-up.

You and Me

Second solo single of South Korean singer, rapper, and Blackpink member Jennie. Jennie said “It’s a song called You & Me. I recorded it about 3~4 years ago “. She also revealed that it was written around the time of her track “SOLO” and that it was on the list of songs that she would release on her solo debut or for something later.
